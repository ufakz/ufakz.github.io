Bookshelf

My book collection is always evolving. I have a habit of diving into several books simultaneously, though sometimes I stumble upon one that completely captivates me until the very last page. Those reads, marked in bold, have profoundly influenced my ideas and how I see the world. Should you decide to check out my collection, I highly recommend starting with those. And of course, if you have any recommendations, I'm all ears!
